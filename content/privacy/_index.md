This notice explains what cezconsulting.com collects and how to control it. CEZ Consulting is the advisory practice of Christos Kotsidimos and is the data controller for this site.

## Analytics and cookies

This site uses Google Analytics 4 to understand how it is used, for example which pages are read and roughly how many people visit. Analytics run only if you click Accept on the cookie banner. Until then, and if you click Decline, no analytics cookies are set and no analytics data is collected. This is enforced with Google Consent Mode, which defaults analytics to denied.

When analytics are enabled, Google sets cookies such as `_ga` and processes pseudonymous usage data, including approximate location derived from your IP address, device and browser type, and the pages you view. It does not identify you by name. Google's handling of this data is covered by [Google's privacy policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

Your consent choice is stored in your browser so the banner does not appear on every visit.

## How to change your choice

To withdraw or change consent, clear this site's data in your browser, which removes the stored choice and the analytics cookies. The banner will appear again on your next visit, and you can choose Decline.

## Information you give us directly

There are no forms or accounts on this site. If you contact us by email, we use your message and email address only to reply and to take any engagement forward. We do not add you to a marketing list.

## Hosting

The site is hosted on GitHub Pages, which may process standard technical information such as IP address in server logs for security and delivery.

## Your rights

Under UK and EU data protection law you can request access to, correction of, or deletion of personal data we hold about you, and you can object to its processing. To make a request, contact us using the address on the [contact page](/contact/).
